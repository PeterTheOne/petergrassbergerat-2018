In April 2011 I helped my Cousin Oliver with the implementation of a Wordpress based Website & Blog for Birgit-Rita Reifferscheidt. Oliver did all the designs, concepts, directing and organization. My part was setting up Wordpress, transforming the designs into HTML and CSS, creating and modifying plugins, added a mootools menu and more. In addition to the site, seven short movies were being made by my brother Stefan, with the help of our good friend Gerd's composition talent.
