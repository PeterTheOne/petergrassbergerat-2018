In the 2.Semester of our Mediatechnology and Design study we took over the Radioshow "Binary Talk" from our Colleagues from the 6.Semester. We aired monthly shows with technology news, event announcements, discussions and of lots of good music. In the beginning it took us up to one full day of recording for one show, later we able to do it in about two hour.
